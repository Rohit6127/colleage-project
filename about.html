About Us:

Royal Education Society-

This college is managed by Royal Education Society, Latur. The Society wants to produce the skilled manpower. The overall control is vested with the Governing Body of Royal Education Society which is comprised of the members such as eminent educationists, software engineers of repute, biotechnologists and well-known social workers, etc. Royal Education Society, looks after the internal coordination and monitoring of the academic and administrative aspects of the college.


Abuot College-

This college is managed by Royal Education Society, Latur, with the mission - Rute Dnyananna Mukti (Liberation is possible with right knowledge).

The institution has an unflinching faith in the spread of state-of-the-art education in the area of the Marathwada region. The College believes in bringing the youth of this region in the mainstream of the modern society.
